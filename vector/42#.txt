SSSSSS Company Salary and Benefit System
company name: SSSSSS

This document is for SSSSSS company's all rules.
I. Purpose
This salary and benefit system aims to establish a fair, reasonable, and competitive compensation system and a comprehensive benefit system to attract, motivate, and retain outstanding talents, and promote the sustainable development of the company.
II. Scope of Application
This system applies to all full - time employees of the company.
III. Salary Structure
Basic Salary: It is determined based on the employee's job value, responsibilities, skill level, and market conditions. It is the basic component of the employee's salary, ensuring the employee's basic living needs. For example, the basic salary of a junior administrative position is 3,000 yuan, and that of a mid - level technical position is 5,000 yuan.
Performance - related Pay: It is linked to the employee's job performance and is paid out after assessment based on factors such as the completion of work goals, work quality, and work attitude. Performance - related pay aims to motivate employees to improve their work performance and create greater value for the company. Generally, performance - related pay accounts for 20% - 40% of the total monthly salary. For example, for a sales employee with a monthly salary of 8,000 yuan, if the performance - related pay accounts for 30%, the performance - related pay is 2,400 yuan. It will be paid according to the assessment of the sales performance completion. If the performance completion rate reaches 120%, the employee can get 1.2 times the performance - related pay, that is, 2,880 yuan.
Allowances and Subsidies:
Post Allowance: For some special positions, such as technical R & D positions and sales positions, a certain post allowance is given according to the particularity and difficulty of the position. For example, a senior R & D engineer receives a monthly post allowance of 1,000 yuan, and a sales supervisor receives a monthly post allowance of 800 yuan.
Transportation Subsidy: The company provides employees with a monthly transportation subsidy of 300 yuan to reduce the transportation burden of employees commuting to and from work.
Meal Subsidy: The company provides employees with a daily meal subsidy of 20 yuan to ensure that employees can get good food security during working hours. Calculated based on 22 working days per month, the meal subsidy is 440 yuan in total.
Communication Subsidy: According to the work needs of employees, a monthly communication subsidy of 200 yuan is given to enable employees to carry out their work better.
IV. Welfare Benefits
Social Insurance: The company pays endowment insurance, medical insurance, unemployment insurance, work - related injury insurance, and maternity insurance for employees in accordance with national laws and regulations, ensuring employees' rights and interests in terms of old - age care, medical treatment, unemployment, work - related injury, and maternity. Taking the lower limit of the local social insurance payment base of 4,000 yuan as an example, the company pays about 800 yuan for endowment insurance, about 360 yuan for medical insurance, about 40 yuan for unemployment insurance, about 20 yuan for work - related injury insurance, and about 20 yuan for maternity insurance for employees every month.
Housing Provident Fund: The company pays housing provident fund for employees at a contribution rate of 12%. Calculated based on an employee's monthly salary of 5,000 yuan, the company pays 600 yuan of housing provident fund for employees every month, helping employees solve housing problems and improve their quality of life.
Paid Annual Leave: Employees who have worked in the company for 1 year can enjoy 5 days of paid annual leave; those who have worked for 3 years can enjoy 7 days of paid annual leave; and those who have worked for 5 years can enjoy 10 days of paid annual leave. Paid annual leave aims to allow employees to have sufficient rest and relaxation outside of work and improve employees' job satisfaction.
Sick Leave: When employees need to take sick leave due to illness, they can enjoy sick leave benefits. During the sick leave period, the company pays a certain proportion of the salary to employees in accordance with national laws, regulations, and company regulations. For example, if the sick leave is within 3 days, the salary is paid at 80% of the normal salary; if the sick leave is more than 3 days but within 10 days, the salary is paid at 60% of the normal salary.
Holiday Benefits: During important festivals such as the Spring Festival, Dragon Boat Festival, and Mid - Autumn Festival, the company distributes holiday gifts or cash benefits to employees. During the Spring Festival, each employee can receive gifts or cash benefits worth 1,000 yuan; during the Dragon Boat Festival, each employee can receive a zongzi gift box worth 300 yuan; during the Mid - Autumn Festival, each employee can receive a mooncake gift box and fruits worth 500 yuan.
Birthday Benefits: The company holds birthday parties for employees or distributes birthday gifts, such as a birthday cake coupon worth 200 yuan or practical daily - use gifts, to make employees feel the warmth and care of the company.
Health Check - ups: The company organizes a comprehensive health check - up for employees every year, with the cost of about 800 yuan per person, paying attention to employees' physical health conditions and safeguarding employees' health.
Training and Development: The company invests 5% of the total employee salary as training funds every year to provide employees with rich training opportunities, including in - house training, external training, and online learning, to help employees improve their professional skills and comprehensive qualities and achieve their personal career development goals. For example, at least 2 professional skill training courses are provided for technical personnel every year, with the cost of each training course being about 5,000 yuan.
V. Salary Adjustment
Annual Salary Adjustment: The company conducts annual salary adjustments based on employees' work performance, market salary levels, and the company's operating conditions every year. Employees with excellent performance (performance rating of A) can have a salary increase of 10% - 15%; employees with good performance (performance rating of B) can have a salary increase of 5% - 8%; employees with average performance (performance rating of C) can have a salary increase of 2% - 3%; employees with poor performance (performance rating of D) may not receive a salary increase or may face salary adjustments. Suppose an employee has an annual salary of 100,000 yuan. If the performance rating is A, the annual salary after the adjustment can reach 110,000 - 115,000 yuan.
Salary Adjustment due to Job Changes: When an employee's job changes, the company will adjust the employee's salary according to the responsibilities, requirements, and market salary levels of the new position. For example, if an ordinary programmer is promoted to a technical supervisor, his monthly salary may be increased from 8,000 yuan to 12,000 yuan.
VI. Salary Payment
Payment Time: The company pays employees' salaries of the previous month in currency on the 15th of each month. In case of holidays or special circumstances, the payment time will be advanced or postponed.
Payment Method: The company pays salaries to employees' personal bank accounts through bank transfer.
VII. Supplementary Provisions
This system is interpreted and revised by the Human Resources Department of the company.
This system comes into effect from the date of issuance. In case of any conflicts with this system, this system shall prevail.
